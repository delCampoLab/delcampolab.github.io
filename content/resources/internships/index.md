### Funding for Internships and Short Stays

#### [JAE Intro](https://jaeintro.csic.es/en/)
* _Description_: The JAE Intro programme offers grants at CSIC centres aimed at university students interested in starting a research career. It provides an introduction to cutting-edge knowledge and scientific praxis at CSIC Institutes.
* _Duration_: Up to 7 months.
* _Eligibility_: Bachelor's and master's students at Spanish universities.
* _Deadline_: Spring.

#### [Erasmus+](https://erasmus-plus.ec.europa.eu/opportunities/opportunities-for-individuals/students/studying-abroad)
* _Description_: Available to Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctoral students. Supports research stays abroad within participating countries.
* _Duration_: Minimum 2 months, maximum 12 months.
* _Deadline_: Set by your home institution.

#### [Rafael del Pino Excellence Scholarship Programme](https://frdelpino.es/en_gb/scholarships/excellence-scholarships/)
* _Description_: The Rafael del Pino Foundation offers scholarships for Spanish graduates to extend their studies at world-class universities and research centres.
* _Duration_: 2 years.
* _Eligibility_: Bachelor's and master's students from Spanish universities.
* _Deadline_: Winter.
