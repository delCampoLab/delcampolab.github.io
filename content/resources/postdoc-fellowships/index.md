If you are interested in joining the lab as a postdoc, please [get in touch](https://delcampolab.com/contact/) to discuss your research interests and which fellowship might be a good fit.

### European & International Fellowships

#### [Beatriu de Pinós Programme](https://agaur.gencat.cat/en/Beatriu-de-Pinos/index.html)
* _Description_: Supports the recruitment of postdoctoral researchers into the Catalan science and technology system. Researchers may freely choose their own research project in any field.
* _Duration_: 3 years.
* _Eligibility_: At least 2 years of postdoctoral experience outside Spain; must have lived or worked outside Spain for at least 2 years in the 3 years prior to the deadline.
* _Deadline_: Mid March.

#### [Juan de la Cierva](https://www.aei.gob.es/en/announcements/announcements-finder/ayudas-contratos-juan-cierva-2022)
* _Description_: Encourages the recruitment of young doctors for postdoctoral training at Spanish R&D centres other than those where they completed their PhD.
* _Duration_: 2 years.
* _Eligibility_: Doctorate obtained within the eligibility window defined by the call.
* _Deadline_: Mid January.

#### [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships)
* _Description_: Supports experienced researchers looking to advance their career through an international research stay.
* _Duration_: 1 to 2 years.
* _Eligibility_: Researchers based in the EU or associated countries, with a PhD and up to 8 years of research experience.
* _Deadline_: Early September.

#### [EMBO Long-Term Postdoctoral Fellowship](http://www.embo.org/funding-awards/fellowships/long-term-fellowships)
* _Description_: Supports excellent postdoctoral researchers in laboratories throughout Europe and the world. International exchange is a key requirement.
* _Duration_: 2 years.
* _Eligibility_: See [EMBO Fellowship guidelines](https://www.embo.org/documents/PDF/Postdoctoral_Fellowships_guidelines.pdf).
* _Deadline_: No deadline (rolling).

#### [HFSP Long-Term Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://www.hfsp.org/funding/hfsp-funding/postdoctoral-fellowships)
* _Description_: For applicants with a PhD in a biological discipline who wish to broaden their expertise with a project significantly different from their previous work.
* _Duration_: 3 years.
* _Eligibility_: See [HFSP eligibility](https://www.hfsp.org/funding/hfsp-funding/postdoctoral-fellowships#eligibility).
* _Deadline_: Second half of August.

#### [HFSP Cross-Disciplinary Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://www.hfsp.org/funding/hfsp-funding/postdoctoral-fellowships)
* _Description_: For applicants with a PhD from outside the life sciences (e.g. physics, chemistry, mathematics, engineering, or computer science) who want to apply their expertise to biology.
* _Duration_: 3 years.
* _Eligibility_: See [HFSP eligibility](https://www.hfsp.org/funding/hfsp-funding/postdoctoral-fellowships#eligibility).
* _Deadline_: Second half of August.

#### [La Caixa Junior Leader Retaining](https://lacaixafoundation.org/en/postdoctoral-junior-leader-fellowships-retaining-call)
* _Description_: "la Caixa" Foundation grants postdoctoral fellowships for researchers of any nationality to carry out a STEM research project in Spain or Portugal.
* _Duration_: 3 years.
* _Eligibility_: PhD obtained between two and seven years prior to the call deadline; at least 6 months of international research stay; must have resided in Spain for more than 12 months in the three preceding years.
* _Deadline_: Fall.

### Country-Specific Fellowships

#### 🇺🇸 USA — [Life Science Research Foundation Award](https://lsrf.org/apply/award-details/)
* _Description_: Provides three years of support to a named postdoctoral researcher at a non-profit university or research institution.
* _Duration_: 3 years.
* _Eligibility_: U.S. citizens only; must apply within 5 years of receiving their PhD.
* _Deadline_: Rolling.

#### 🇯🇵 Japan — [Canon Foundation Research Fellowships](https://www.canonfoundation.org/programmes/research-fellowships/)
* _Description_: Grants up to 15 fellowships annually to European and Japanese researchers for research exchanges between Japan and Europe.
* _Duration_: 3 months to 1 year.
* _Eligibility_: Japanese researchers coming to Europe; Master's or PhD obtained within the last ten years.
* _Deadline_: September.

#### 🇸🇪 Sweden — [Swedish Research Council International Postdoc](https://www.vr.se/english.html)
* _Description_: Gives newly qualified researchers from Swedish universities the opportunity to expand their networks and competences by working abroad.
* _Duration_: 18 months to 3 years.
* _Eligibility_: Doctoral degree from a Swedish university, completed no more than 2 years before the grant period starts.
* _Deadline_: February (spring call) or September (fall call).
