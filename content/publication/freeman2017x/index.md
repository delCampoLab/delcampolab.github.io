abstract: "X-cells have long been associated with tumor-like formations (xenomas) in marine fish, including many commercially important species. Despite decades of awareness, the phylogenetic identity of X-cells has remained unknown. Here we use single-cell transcriptomics and genomics to establish that X-cells are alveolates closely related to apicomplexans, forming a previously uncharacterized lineage we call Chromosphaera perkinsii."
